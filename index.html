About Our Gym

HealthClub Tral was established in 2024 with a mission to create a welcoming fitness environment for people of all levels. Our facility spans 12,000 square feet and features modern equipment for cardio, strength training, and functional fitness.

What sets us apart is our commitment to personalized fitness. Our team of certified trainers works with members to develop customized programs that align with their goals, whether it's weight loss, muscle gain, or improved health.

We believe fitness is a journey, not a destination. That's why we focus on building sustainable habits and providing the support and resources our members need to succeed long-term.
